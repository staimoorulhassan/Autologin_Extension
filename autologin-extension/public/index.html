Features

Everything you need for bulk login automation

Built for power users who need to authenticate across dozens or hundreds of accounts reliably.

🤖

AI Vision Detection

Takes a live screenshot and asks an AI model what step the login is on and what the field selectors are. Works on pages it has never seen before.

⚡

Batch Processing

Queue hundreds of accounts and process them sequentially. Configurable delay between accounts. Pause and resume at any time.

🧩

Multi-Step Flows

Handles email-first flows like Google, Microsoft, Yahoo, and LinkedIn. Up to 6 steps per account — enough for email → CAPTCHA → password → 2FA.

🍪

Cookie Capture

After every successful login, all session cookies are saved to your Downloads folder as a text file. Useful for session replay or auditing.

🛡️

CAPTCHA Handling

Checkbox reCAPTCHA clicked automatically. Text and math CAPTCHAs solved by AI. Image grid CAPTCHAs pause for human intervention.

📊

Per-Account Logs

Every attempt logged with status, timestamp, and error detail. Export as CSV or JSON. Full history retained in encrypted IndexedDB.

🧠

DOM Fallback

If AI is not configured, falls back to DOM-based detection using selector chains, visibility filtering, and React/Angular native input setter.

🕵️

Human-Like Behavior

Random per-character typing delays. Mouse events fired in correct sequence. Tabs opened in background. Cookies cleared between accounts.

📥

Easy Import

Paste credentials in CSV format or colon-delimited (url:username:password). Import hundreds of accounts in seconds via the Options page.

How It Works

From zero to logged in

No selectors to write. No per-site setup. The AI figures it out.

1

Import your credentials

Open Options and paste your accounts in CSV or colon-delimited format. The extension stores them encrypted in IndexedDB on your device.

2

Press Start Batch Login

The extension opens each site in a background tab, waits for it to load, then takes a screenshot.

3

AI reads the page

The screenshot is sent to Pollinations (gemini-fast) with a structured prompt. The AI returns the login step, CSS selectors for each field, and whether a CAPTCHA is present.

4

Fields filled and submitted

Username and password typed with realistic delays. Submit clicked. The extension waits for navigation and checks whether login succeeded.

5

Cookies saved, account logged out

On success, all session cookies are captured and saved. The extension then logs out and clears cookies before moving to the next account.

CAPTCHA Support

Handles CAPTCHAs automatically where possible

Security

Your credentials never leave your device

Everything is stored locally, encrypted. No server. No sync. No cloud.

🔒 Encrypted at rest

Passwords are encrypted with XSalsa20-Poly1305 (the same algorithm used by Signal) before being written to IndexedDB. The key is derived from your browser profile — without your browser, the data cannot be decrypted.

📡 No remote storage

All credentials, cookies, and logs are stored in IndexedDB on your local device only. The extension makes no calls to any server except the AI provider you configure and the sites you're logging into.

🔑 API key security

Your AI provider key is stored in chrome.storage.local — encrypted by the browser on disk. It is never hardcoded in source and never committed to the repository.

⚠️ Honest risk assessment

The extension has access to all pages you visit (required for content scripts) and can read page content. Review the open source code before use. Use for accounts you own and have authority to automate.
